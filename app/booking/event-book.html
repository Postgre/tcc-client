Important Information

Booking Requirments

The creation of an account and either a 50% deposit or a full payment is required to book carolers for your event. If your event is more than 30 days from today's date, you can pay a minimum 50% deposit to book your event. If your event is less than 30 days from today's date, you will need to make a full payment to book the event.

Payment Options

You will have the option of paying with a credit card (charges apply) or by check (no charge). Again, an event is not booked until a payment has been received and processed.

Event Cancellation and Refund Policy

The Christmas Carolers reserves the right to cancel any event, for any reason and at any time with a full refund provided to the customer. Customers who cancel an event 30+ days from the date of the event will receive a full refund of all monies paid to-date. Customers who cancel an event within 30 days of the date of the event will forfeit all monies paid to-date.

General and Liability Coverage

The Christmas Carolers carry $1,000,000 general liability coverage as well as professional liability coverage.

National Carolers Association

Each of our carolers are members of the National Carolers Association ensuring our commitment to quality music and costumes.
