Travel Expenses

The Christmas Carolers will pay $1 per mile over 30 miles of travel ( to the driver ) from the address you provide. We will also pay carolers for any event taking longer than 30 minutes to get to. We will pay carolers $10 per hour for travel time + any approved expensess prior to the date of the event.

The lead caroler on each event will be asked to provide an estimate of travel time and any other cost associated with travel ( parking, toll-roads, etc. ). Please decide for your group the address for which the 30 miles should be centered upon.
