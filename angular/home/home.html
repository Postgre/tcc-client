ABOUT OUR CAROLERS

The Christmas Carolers recruitees highly skilled voice students from the finest music schools in your area. Carolers are dressed in finely appointed Vitorian-styled costumes and are trained to entertain your guest as they arrive and move through your party or event

The Christmas Carolers are required to be a member of the National Carolers Association; which ensures a level of quality assurance. The National Carolers Associations provides carolers with quality costumes, approved musical arrangements and liability insurance
